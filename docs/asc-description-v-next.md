## Full new description (paste this into ASC en-US description field for the new draft version)

WalkCue is the walking timer for people who just want to walk — without the fuss.

Tap start, get a guided walk with simple interval cues, and build a consistent daily habit. Pick a routine, set a goal, and go.

Features
• Quick Start — one tap, guided walk with warm up, brisk, and cool down
• Interval cues — haptic and audio cues at each transition
• Preset routines — beginner, brisk intervals, treadmill, recovery
• Daily goal tracking — minutes walked, sessions completed
• Walk history — see your consistency over time
• Walk reminders — simple, reliable local notifications
• Background completion alerts — notifications fire reliably even when the app is backgrounded, so your walk completion is signaled the moment it's predicted to finish
• Works offline, large readable UI, one-handed use

Privacy-first
• No account, no sign-in
• No cloud sync · anonymous, opt-out product analytics only

Pricing
• Free forever: quick start, built-in routines, one custom routine, 7-day history
• Premium Monthly — $1.99/month (auto-renewing): unlimited custom routines, full history, unlimited reminders
• Premium Yearly — $14.99/year (auto-renewing) with a 7-day free trial: the same premium features, billed annually
• Premium Lifetime — $8.99 (one-time): the same premium features, paid once, kept forever

Pick yearly with a 7-day free trial, monthly to try premium month-to-month, or lifetime to unlock everything with a single purchase.

Subscription details
Payment will be charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period; renewal is charged within 24 hours of the period end. Manage subscriptions in your Apple ID Account Settings. Any unused portion of a free trial is forfeited when you purchase a subscription before the trial ends.
