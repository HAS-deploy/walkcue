WalkCue — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-17

WalkCue is a walking timer app. It runs entirely on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not operate any servers. WalkCue does not transmit or store any personal data anywhere other than your own device.

What lives on your device

Your settings, saved routines, and walk history are stored locally using standard iOS preferences and local storage. Uninstalling the app deletes this data.

Network activity

The only network activity WalkCue performs is with Apple's App Store for the optional in-app purchase (the one-time Lifetime Unlock). Apple processes that transaction; we do not see your payment details.

Notifications

If you enable a walk reminder, WalkCue schedules a local iOS notification. Nothing about the reminder is sent to us or anyone else.

Anonymous usage analytics

WalkCue uses PostHog to collect anonymous usage events — screen views, taps on key features, and basic crash and performance signals — so we can see which parts of the app are useful and which need work. Events are tied to a random per-install identifier and are not linked to your name, email, Apple ID, or any contact details (we don't collect those). Walk durations, distances, and goal entries are never sent. The app contains no advertising SDKs and does not request the App Tracking Transparency permission.
